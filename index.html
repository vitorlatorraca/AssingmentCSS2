Introduction

The song "Céu Azul" by Charlie Brown Jr. is a tribute to love and gratitude for the mother.

Interpretation

The song expresses a deep love and respect for the mother, acknowledging her as an essential and inspiring figure in the singer's life. The verses evoke feelings of gratitude, love, and protection.
